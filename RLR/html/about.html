ABOUT

RAIN LIGHT RACING

Built on a passion for racing and community, Rain Light Racing has grown into one of the leading sim racing leagues across Australia and New Zealand.

Our Story

Rain Light Racing is an Australia and New Zealand–based sim racing league built around competitive, community-driven motorsport. Founded by Crossy and Gunthar, it evolved from years of racing across established communities into a dedicated league of its own.

Our Growth

Originally formed through the “Monday Night Madness” streams, the series has grown into a structured championship with live broadcasts, active management, and a growing driver base.

What We Race

Now competing on Le Mans Ultimate, Rain Light Racing primarily runs LMGT3 and Hypercar racing focused on fair, accessible, and competitive racing.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the sport of sim racing, foster a strong community of drivers, and provide exciting racing experiences throughout the year.
